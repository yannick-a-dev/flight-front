Create Flight

Flight number is required and must be between 3 and 10 characters.
Departure time is required and must be a valid date in the past or present.
Arrival time is required and must be a valid date in the future.
Departure airport is required and must be exactly 3 characters.
Arrival airport is required and must be exactly 3 characters.
Status is required and must be between 1 and 50 characters.
Reservations list is required.
Alerts list is required.
